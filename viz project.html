Lot Area vs House Price in Neighborhood

-
The size of dots indicates the average of lot area in square feet of neighborhood. The color intensity indicates the average price. The darker the shade the higher the house sale price in the neighborhood. By hovering on the dots, the details of the neighborhood can be obtained.
The scatter plot help us to see the relationship between Lot Area and Sale Price. With the appearance of regression, the relationship can be translated and understood clearly. The intercept indicates the starting price of houses in a neighborhood. Meanwhile, the slope indicates the rising price per square feet.
